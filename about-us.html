About Vintage Car Service

Welcome to Vintage Car Service, the ideal place for the care and restoration of classic and vintage cars. With a special passion for preserving automotive history, we provide exceptional service to ensure your cherished vehicle remains in excellent condition.

Our History

Founded in 2015, Vintage Car Service started as a small workshop dedicated to the restoration and maintenance of vintage cars. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for our attention to detail and commitment to quality. Our skilled technicians have many years of experience working with classic vehicles, ensuring each car receives the care it deserves.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver outstanding service and restore the beauty of classic cars while preserving their heritage for future generations. We aim to exceed our clients' expectations by providing high-quality work and personalized care. Every car that passes through our doors is treated with the utmost respect and expertise.

Meet the Team
